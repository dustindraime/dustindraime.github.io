SERVICES

My goal is to take simple home maintenance items off your plate so you can work on doing things you enjoy. My primary focus centers on installation of items or minor repairs of existing ones. I am also open to helping with other odd jobs around your home.

Examples of services include:

  1. Light fixture installation
  2. Ceiling fan installation
  3. Appliance installation
  4. Garbage disposal installation
  5. Curtain and towel rod installation
  6. Faucet Installation
  7. TV mounting
  8. Unclogging toilets and drains
  9. Toilet installation and repair
  10. Baby proofing homes (cabinet locks, baby gates, securing furniture)
  11. Furniture assembly (including hauling or pickup)
  12. Picture hanging
  13. Light bulb replacement
  14. Smoke detector replacement & battery installation
  15. Gutter cleaning
  16. Pressure washing/Trash can cleaning
  17. Small to medium tree/shrub trimming and removal
  18. Garbage/debris removal
  19. Yardwork
  20. Any other odd jobs….please ask!
How It Works

For basic items, I generally work on a per hour basis. I can usually install the materials you purchase or I can also procure materials for reimbursement. There is no mark-up on materials or products. My hourly rate is $100.00 depending upon the type of service requested.

**Some jobs may vary in price based on complexity and type of labor.

Integrity First

I am a registered LLC. and insured for your protection. I am NOT a general contractor, certified plumber or licensed electrician. However, most basic home repairs do not require those licenses in Texas. If your job requires a level of service outside of my scope, I will recommend that you seek out the right professional for the job.
